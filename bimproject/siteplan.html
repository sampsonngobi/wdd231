SITE NAME:

BRASILIA IMPORT MARKET

Reason

My project is about the import market in Brasilia DF, Brazil

SITE PURPOSE

The website will provide general information about the import market as well as information about registered service providers, ranging from computer repair and software stores to vendors of computer, gaming filming and other gargets. The website will also provide a platform for users to register and post their services and products for sale. A user can easily find a service provider or vendor by browsing through the categories or searching for a specific service or product.

Scenarios

  • What happens in the import market?
  • What are the services and products available in the import market?
  • How can a user find a service provider or vendor?
  • What are the best service providers based on ratings from clients
